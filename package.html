Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our health packages

What is included in the health packages?

Our health packages include comprehensive blood tests, vital signs check, doctor consultation, diagnostic tests (based on package), and a detailed health report. Higher packages include additional tests like ECG, ultrasound, and specialized consultations.

How long does the health checkup take?

Basic packages take 2-3 hours, standard packages 3-4 hours, and premium packages 4-5 hours. This includes all tests, consultations, and breaks. Fasting is required for 8-10 hours before the checkup for accurate results.

When will I get my reports?

Most test reports are available within 24-48 hours. You'll receive digital reports via email and WhatsApp. A follow-up consultation with a doctor is included to explain the reports and provide health recommendations.

Do you provide home sample collection?

Yes, we provide free home sample collection for standard and premium packages. Our trained phlebotomists collect samples at your preferred time. Basic packages require hospital visit for sample collection.

Can I customize a package?

Yes, we offer customized packages based on your specific health needs, age, gender, and medical history. Contact our health advisors for a personalized package quote.
