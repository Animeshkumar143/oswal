Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment in three ways: 1) Call our helpline +91 83765 72523, 2) Use the online booking form on our website, or 3) Visit our hospital directly. We recommend booking online for faster service.

What are your emergency service hours?

Our emergency services are available 24/7, including holidays. No prior appointment is needed for emergency cases. Just walk in or call our emergency helpline for immediate assistance.

Do you accept insurance claims?

Yes, we accept all major insurance providers including Star Health, ICICI Lombard, HDFC Ergo, and more. We provide cashless facility for network hospitals. Please bring your insurance card and ID proof during your visit.

Can I get online consultation?

Yes, we offer online video consultations with our specialist doctors. You can book a virtual appointment through our website or mobile app. Prescriptions and lab test recommendations are provided digitally.

What safety measures do you follow?

We follow strict COVID-19 safety protocols including regular sanitization, mandatory masks, temperature checks, social distancing in waiting areas, and regular testing of staff. Our facilities are equipped with HEPA filters and UV sterilization.
